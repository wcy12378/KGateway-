# Product

## Register

product

## Users

KAgent serves two related audiences inside an enterprise. Employees use the AI workspace for daily questions, knowledge retrieval, and guided Agent workflows. Platform operators use the management area to inspect runtime health, traces, breaker state, Prompt versions, and tool audit records.

The primary task is getting a trustworthy answer quickly. Operational surfaces support diagnosis and governance without turning the employee experience into a monitoring console.

## Product Purpose

KAgent is an enterprise AI assistant and governance platform. It brings conversations, internal knowledge, multi-Agent workflows, provider routing, memory, observability, and safety controls into one coherent product.

Success means employees can work without understanding infrastructure, while operators can verify what happened, trace failures, and control risk without leaving the product.

## Brand Personality

Calm, precise, trustworthy.

The product should feel quietly capable and familiar. Its language is concise, primarily Chinese, and operationally honest. It should carry the reading comfort of Notion, the state precision of Linear, and the collaborative familiarity of Slack without cloning any of them.

## Anti-references

- A technical monitoring dashboard presented as the whole product.
- A generic AI demo with purple gradients, glowing surfaces, and theatrical animation.
- A dark game-like console or cyberpunk control room.
- Card walls, oversized marketing headings, and decorative status indicators.
- Raw chain-of-thought or hidden model reasoning exposed as a feature.
- Unfamiliar controls that replace standard product affordances for visual novelty.

## Design Principles

1. **The answer is the product.** Keep the employee workspace focused on readable content and clear next actions.
2. **Progress must be verifiable.** Show tools, sources, duration, and status instead of hidden reasoning.
3. **Separate work from operations.** Keep employee tasks and management controls distinct within one consistent shell.
4. **Density follows the job.** Conversation surfaces breathe; tables and diagnostics stay compact and scannable.
5. **State changes are explicit.** Every loading, success, warning, failure, and destructive action has a clear textual and visual treatment.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG 2.2 AA. All primary workflows must support keyboard navigation, visible focus, readable contrast, reduced motion, and zoom up to 200%. Color is never the only carrier of status. Chinese typography and mixed Chinese-Latin content must remain legible at standard product sizes.
